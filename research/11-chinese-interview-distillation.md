## Key Chinese Renderings of CZ's Mental Models

### 1. Efficiency as Core Identity
- "我是一个受效率驱动的人，这是下意识的" — "I am an efficiency-driven person, this is subconscious"
- Context: Describing his work on order execution systems. The Chinese phrasing "下意识的" (subconscious) emphasizes this as a deeply embedded trait, not a learned skill.

### 2. Three Fundamental Technologies
- "我的生活中存在着两种根本性的技术。那就是互联网...还有这个比特币...接下来发生的事情将是15年之后了...那就是人工智能" — "There are two/three fundamental technologies in my life: Internet, Bitcoin, and AI"
- "我当时35、36岁。我没有。我不会错过它的" — "I was 35-36. I would not miss it."
- The Chinese "不会错过" carries stronger emotional determination than "won't miss" in English.

### 3. Selling the Apartment for Bitcoin
- "我要把我在上海的公寓卖掉，然后去买比特币" — "I'm going to sell my Shanghai apartment and buy Bitcoin"
- "平均下来大约是600美元" — averaged ~$600/BTC
- This story is told identically to the English version, including the detail of receiving payments in batches.

### 4. On Wealth and Simplicity
- "我不关心花哨的东西。我不关心风格。我不关心颜色。我不关心闪闪发光。镀金的，只要它功能正常，我就没事" — "I don't care about flashy things. I don't care about style. Color. Sparkle. Gold-plated. As long as it functions, I'm fine."
- "客厅差不多每个月都会漏水，因为这房子太旧了" — "The living room leaks about every month because the house is too old"
- "它是我买的第三手或第四手的旧房子" — "It's a third or fourth-hand old house I bought"

### 5. North Star Metric
- "我认为是真正的活跃用户数。不是交易量。不是收入" — "Real active users. Not trading volume. Not revenue."
- "只要有更多的人想使用它，您就拥有价值" — "As long as more people want to use it, you have value"

### 6. Emotional Stability as Superpower
- "我很平静" — "I am calm" (direct self-description)
- "你的振幅非常适中。它很窄" — Chamath describing CZ: "Your amplitude is very moderate. It's narrow."
- "我不是超级自大的" — "I'm not super arrogant" / "我知道我的弱点" — "I know my weaknesses"

### 7. Resignation from Binance
- "辞职真的，真的非常艰难。我当时都哭出来了。我真正有这种感觉的另一次，是几年前我父亲去世的时候" — "Resigning was really, really hard. I cried. The only other time I truly felt this way was when my father passed away years ago."
- This emotional moment is rendered identically in both versions.

### 8. DOJ Negotiation Psychology
- "两周是最佳时间。因为时间再长一点，你真的就习惯了" — "Two weeks is the optimal time [for silence]. Any longer, you really get used to it."
- "他们非常擅长这种脑力劳动" — "They are very skilled at this mental labor" (describing DOJ negotiation tactics)

### 9. Prison Experience
- "独善其身就好。保持安静。避人耳目" — "Keep to yourself. Stay quiet. Stay out of sight." (prison advice)
- "欢迎加入我们的车队/团体" — The racial grouping system described identically

### 10. On Children and Parenting
- "我希望他们过上健康快乐的生活，无论他们如何定义这种生活" — "I hope they live healthy and happy lives, however they define that life"
- "不要伤害自己。不要伤害别人" — "Don't hurt yourself. Don't hurt others." (his parents' teaching)
- "很不'中国式'" — "Very un-'Chinese'" (describing his parents' non-pushy parenting)

### 11. Life Philosophy (Closing)
- "你不需要超级聪明也能成功。你不能太笨，但你也不需要超级聪明" — "You don't need to be super smart to succeed. You can't be too dumb, but you don't need to be super smart."
- "你想把自己推到像120%, 110%, 130%这样的范围" — "Push yourself to 110-130%"
- "如果你这样做30年，并且运气好，你很可能会相对成功" — "If you do this for 30 years and get lucky, you'll likely be relatively successful"

### 12. Spider Web Metaphor for Life
- "我倾向于想象一个蜘蛛网图，金钱只是其中一个切线" — "I tend to imagine a spider web diagram, money is just one tangent line"
- "还有健康，还有家庭。还有其他能让你快乐的事情，比如你的价值观、贡献、积极影响" — "There's also health, family, and other things that make you happy -- your values, contributions, positive impact"
