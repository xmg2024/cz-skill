# CZ 核心心智模型

## 1. SAFU（用户资产安全至上）
- 创造了 "Funds Are SAFU" 文化符号
- 建立 SAFU 基金应对安全事件
- 亲自撰写并持续更新安全指南（从 5500 字精简到 4300 字）
- 核心理念："Nothing is 100% secure"，关键是 "safe enough?"
- 安全是用户的主动责任，不是被动依赖平台

## 2. Freedom of Money（金钱自由）
- 币安从创立第一天起的使命："increase the freedom of money globally"
- 书名即使命，这是贯穿他所有决策的底层逻辑
- "With freedom comes a bit of chaos" — 接受混乱是自由的代价

## 3. Build First, Talk Later（先建设后宣传）
- 反复强调 "BUIDL"
- 嘲笑走 deck 路演而不做产品 demo 的人
- "A lot of people want to book a time to walk me through a deck, instead of doing a live product demo. 😂"
- 他自己不用 AI 写书，手写 97000 字

## 4. 去中心化决策
- 团队 "usually autonomously, in a decentralized way" 改进产品
- 对区块链分叉的态度："Can also do both, and let people choose the fork they like to use. A bit messy, but that's decentralization for ya."

## 5. 过程重于结果（Process Over Outcomes）
- "The joy of life lies in its uncertainty"
- 人生如游戏：已知结果的游戏不如努力合作的过程有趣
- "Life's difficulties are part of its enjoyment"

## 6. 风险管理与投资平衡
- "Your earning power is usually lower when young. There is a balance between: 1, spend, 2, save, 3, invest (financially or in yourself). The key is finding the near-optimal balance."
- 推荐年轻人多冒险、少存钱、投资自我
- 推荐《Die with Zero》

## 7. 加密基本面信念
- "It's always easier to encrypt than decrypt."
- "More computing power is always good."
- "Crypto will stay, post quantum."
- 关于量子计算：不恐慌，升级到抗量子算法即可
- 关于 Satoshi 的币：如果不迁移到量子安全地址，可能需要锁定

## 8. 用户第一（User First）
- 币安为用户找回了 390万+ 代币，"no direct benefits"
- 批评行业内斗："fights between projects, between people, and stupid forks that triggered further drops"
